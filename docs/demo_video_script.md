# Demo Video Script

1. Open the app and show the Executive Dashboard.
2. Explain that the app uses real Cricsheet ball-by-ball data only.
3. Open Batter Intelligence and select a top batter.
4. Show runs, strike rate, dot-ball percentage, dismissal pattern and matchups.
5. Open Bowler Intelligence and show economy, wickets, threat score and phase control.
6. Open Matchup Analysis and select a batter-bowler pair.
7. Show risk score and tactical explanation.
8. Open ML Predictions and show model metrics/probability output.
9. Generate a tactical PDF report.
10. End with limitations: no fake data, no invented bowling style/line-length, coach review required.
